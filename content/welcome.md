# Welcome to NSG

**NSG (Nousagi Gang)**
*A Filipino gaming community exploring text-based worlds together.*

---

## Who We Are

NSG is a community of Filipino players united by a shared love for text-based MMORPGs.

The group began with a simple idea: to create a place where Filipino players could find each other, cooperate in-game, and build friendships. From that idea, Nousagi Gang was founded and later became widely known simply as **NSG**.

Over time, the community expanded beyond a single game and continues to grow as members explore new worlds together.

More than just a gang tag, NSG is a place where gamers support one another, share experiences, and enjoy the journey across different games.

---

## Our Motto

*“Di palulupig, Di pasisiil.
United by games, bound by values.”*

## Current Games

NSG members can be found across several text-based MMORPGs.
