To all current and former members of Nousagi Gang PH,

We have some unfortunate news to share.

On June 5, 2026, it was discovered that the **Nousagi Gang** in **CyberCode Online** had been dissolved due to insufficient upkeep resources and funds after a long period of inactivity.

![Screenshot from CCO PH Discord server of Chiiims](https://nsg.pages.dev/screenshots/back-and-rebuilding/Screenshot_20260605_Discord.jpg "Screenshot from CCO PH Discord server of Chiiims")

<small>*(**Disclaimer:** To respect the privacy of our members and friends, some screenshots shown in this announcement have been edited. Usernames, profile pictures, IDs, timestamps, and other personal or sensitive information may have been redacted, blurred, cropped, or modified. These changes were made solely for privacy purposes and do not affect the authenticity or context of the events being documented.)*</small>

The discovery was first made by **BLACK_ACE**, who noticed that he was no longer part of the gang and announced in CCO's Tagalog chat. **Chiiims** of **Meows_Cafe [8_8]** informed members through CCO PH Discord server, and [Homongji](/members/jude) later confirmed that the gang had indeed been dissolved.

For many of us, this was heartbreaking news.

![Screenshot from CCO PH Discord server of Chiiims](https://nsg.pages.dev/screenshots/back-and-rebuilding/Screenshot_20260608_Discord.jpg "Screenshot from CCO PH Discord server of Chiiims")

**Nousagi [NSG]** was founded on February 23, 2022, and became home to countless memories, friendships, victories, and adventures. Though activity gradually declined as members became busy with work, studies, family, and other real-life responsibilities, the community itself remained close to our hearts.

When I learned of the dissolution on June 8, I immediately informed our founder, **Skae_19**. Like many of us, he was saddened by the news. We also attempted to contact our leader, [En_Roise](/members/en_roise), but unfortunately have not yet received a response.

![Screenshot from K4LM3D and Skae_19 Discord chat messages](https://nsg.pages.dev/screenshots/back-and-rebuilding/Screenshot_20260608_Discord1.jpg "Screenshot from K4LM3D and Skae_19 Discord chat messages")

The dissolution of Nousagi Gang in CyberCode Online marks the end of an important chapter in our history. However, after reflecting on everything this community has meant to us, we realized something important:

Nousagi Gang PH was never just a gang.\
It was never just a tag beside our names.\
It was never just a roster in a game.\
It has always been a community built by the people who believed in it.\
Because of this, we have decided not to let the story end here.

Today, June 16, 2026, with the support and commitment of [Xythran](/members/xythran), we have begun rebuilding Nousagi Gang in CyberCode Online from the ground up.

This will not be easy, but we believe the spirit of NSG is worth preserving.

While **En_Roise** remains inactive, I, [K4LM3D](/members/k4lm3d), will temporarily help coordinate and lead these rebuilding efforts until she returns or the community decides on the best path forward.

**This rebuilding effort is not about replacing the past.\
It is about honoring it.\
It is about preserving the friendships, memories, and values that made NSG special in the first place.**

To all former members:\
The doors of NSG remain open.

Whether you are still active in CCO, playing ALC, exploring other games, or simply checking in after a long absence, we would be happy to see you again.

Let us rebuild together.

Di palulupig, Di pasisiil.\
United by games, bound by values.

&mdash;[K4LM3D](/members/k4lm3d) 2026.06.16

  *A Legacy Beyond the Game*
  
  Although the original NSG chapter in CyberCode Online came to an end, its story is far from over. The gang has since been re-established, and efforts are currently underway to restore its upkeep resources and rebuild its foundation.
  
  The memories, friendships, events, contests, late-night grinding sessions, and random conversations that brought us together remain an important part of our history. As these reflections show, NSG was never just a gang—it was a community that connected people from different places and backgrounds through a shared passion for gaming.
  
  As we begin this new chapter, we carry forward the spirit of NSG and the friendships that made it special. We look forward to creating new memories together and welcoming both returning and new members into the community once again.
