As Nousagi [NSG] grew from a small gang into a community spanning multiple games, the members recognized the importance of maintaining shared values and respectful conduct.

To guide members across different gaming worlds, NSG established community standards and internal practices.

- [Gaming Guidelines](/gaming-guidelines) – A set of principles that promote fair play, respect among members, and responsible behavior in the games where NSG operates.
- [HOP Protocol](/hop-protocol) – A framework that helps maintain order, cooperation, and coordination within the community.

These guidelines help ensure that wherever NSG members play, they represent the community with integrity and unity.

Together with the motto:

> *“Di palulupig, Di pasisiil. United by games, bound by values.”*

They serve as the foundation of NSG’s identity.
