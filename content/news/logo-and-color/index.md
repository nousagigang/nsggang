Introducing our official logo and color! 🎨

After some tweaks and refinements, we’re proud to unveil our official logo.

## The Logo
- **The Hood**: Represents the persona we adopt during our gaming adventures.
- **The Rabbit**: Symbolizes the collective strength and unity of the Nousagi Gang.
- **Fierce and Tempered**: A reminder of our unwavering spirit and commitment to our core values and code of conduct.

## The Color: Neon Orange 🧡 
This vibrant shade perfectly complements our new logo and embodies the spirit of the Nousagi Gang.

*What does Neon Orange symbolize?*
- **Enthusiasm and Confidence**: Just like our community!
- **Fun, Playfulness, and Creativity**: Reflecting our gaming adventures.
- **Assertiveness**: A bold statement of our presence.

Let’s all embrace this new identity and show our community pride! ✊
