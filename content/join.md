# Join NSG

🙏 Thank you for your interest in **Nousagi Gang**.

NSG is a Filipino gaming community focused on cooperation, friendship, and shared adventures in text-based MMORPGs. While we are a relaxed group, we value respect, teamwork, and positive conduct in the games we play.

## Who Can Join

Players who wish to join NSG should:

- Be respectful to fellow members and other players
- Follow the **NSG Gaming Guidelines**
- Be willing to cooperate with the community
- Enjoy playing text-based MMORPGs with others

<!-- While NSG was originally formed to unite Filipino players, we welcome anyone who respects our community and values. -->

## How to Join

If you are interested in joining NSG:

1. Contact an existing NSG member in-game.
2. Introduce yourself and the game you play.
3. A member or leader may guide you through the process of joining.

Recruitment may vary depending on the game.

## A Friendly Reminder

NSG is more than just a gang tag. It is a community built on friendship and shared experiences.

If you share those values, you are always welcome to walk the path with us.

> *“Di palulupig, Di pasisiil. United by games, bound by values.”*
