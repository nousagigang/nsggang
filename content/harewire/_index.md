## Information

**Harewire** is a bot for Nousagi Gang HQ. It has an internal alert system used by the gang's **SysOps**. When activated, it instantly delivers urgent messages to all gang members across the server.

### Functions
- 🔔 To alert the gang about critical information
- 📩 To direct message gang members
- 💼 To store data about Nousagi Gang
- 📄 To document happenings about the gang by writing logs
